Fit Fusion Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024
Effective Date: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fit Fusion ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services, including our integration with third-party health and fitness devices such as Whoop.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, date of birth, gender, height, weight, fitness goals
  • Health Data: Body composition, workout logs, nutrition tracking, sleep patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers

2.2 Third-Party Health Data (Whoop Integration)

When you connect your Whoop device to Fit Fusion, we may collect:

  • Recovery Data: Recovery scores, heart rate variability (HRV), resting heart rate
  • Sleep Data: Sleep duration, sleep stages, sleep efficiency, sleep consistency
  • Strain Data: Daily strain scores, kilojoules burned, heart rate zones
  • Workout Data: Exercise activities, heart rate during workouts, performance metrics
  • Body Measurements: Height, weight, maximum heart rate (as provided by Whoop)

2.3 Usage Data

  • App usage patterns, features accessed, time spent in the app
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Device logs and technical information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Provide personalized fitness and health recommendations
  • Track and analyze your fitness progress and goals
  • Generate insights and analytics about your health and fitness
  • Sync and integrate data from connected devices (like Whoop)
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

3.2 Whoop Data Usage

  • Display your daily recovery, strain, and sleep scores
  • Calculate comprehensive calorie burn including metabolism and exercise
  • Provide weekly metabolism estimates and health insights
  • Integrate Whoop data with your overall fitness tracking

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your data
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate our app (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4.3 Whoop Data Sharing

Data from your Whoop device is only shared with Whoop as necessary to provide the integration service. We do not share your Whoop data with other third parties without your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • View and update your personal information
  • Download your data in a portable format
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities

7.2 Whoop Integration Controls

  • Connect or disconnect your Whoop device at any time
  • Control which Whoop data is shared with Fit Fusion
  • Revoke access to your Whoop account through our app

8. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services including:

  • Whoop: For health and fitness data collection
  • Supabase: For data storage and backend services
  • Stripe: For payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during such transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in our app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
